Chairman Message

Ambrosia Shipping Ltd., headquartered in Chittagong, Bangladesh, is now expanding its footprint to Dhaka. Established in 2008 by Mohammed Shah Alam, Board of Director, Bangladesh Freight Forwarders Association (BAFFA), Ambrosia Shipping Ltd. is a leading logistics provider offering comprehensive end-to-end services across the country. With an extensive network of its own offices in Bangladesh, the company specializes in Ocean Freight (LCL & FCL), Air Freight, and 3PL services.

In addition, through the Shah Amanat International Airport in Chittagong, Ambrosia Shipping provides efficient air freight solutions. The company is also fully capable of offering customs brokerage and complete logistics support, ensuring a seamless experience for its clients.
